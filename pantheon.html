Pantheon

The Pantheon, located in the lively center of Rome, is a breathtaking building that has amazed people for centuries. Originally built as a temple for the Roman gods, its huge dome is a true marvel, featuring a round opening at the top, called the oculus, which lets in beautiful sunlight and creates a warm atmosphere inside. The grand entrance is lined with impressive columns made from stunning Egyptian granite, inviting everyone to explore its rich history. Today, the Pantheon is not just a historical site; it’s surrounded by lively cafes and street performers in Piazza della Rotonda, making it a perfect spot to relax and soak in the vibrant spirit of Rome.
