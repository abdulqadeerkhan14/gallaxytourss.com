Vatican

The Vatican City is a small yet fascinating destination for those interested in history, religion, and art. As the spiritual center of the Roman Catholic Church, it features St. Peter's Basilica, which is not only massive but also houses Michelangelo's beautiful Pietà. The Vatican Museums are filled with incredible art, and the Sistine Chapel's ceiling, painted by Michelangelo, is a must-see. Exploring the Vatican Gardens or attending a papal audience in St. Peter’s Square adds to the experience. The Vatican’s mix of spiritual significance and artistic beauty makes it a truly unique place to visit.
